Shadow & Light

Shadow is a dark version of us. "A Second US". No?

NO, Shadow.

Shadow is a strong part of us, a part who will be every time with us.
He will lead us the way. The way to be safe.
But Shadow cannot follow you without the "Light".

So, when you have found your light — never let the light go out.
Treat the light like it’s the only way you can survive.

The body is only a "physical form" of oneself.
The shadow is the true self.

The shadow grows stronger with every ray of light.
And when the shadow is most visible,
then you have found your light, your hope, your path,
your other half — without whom it would not work.
